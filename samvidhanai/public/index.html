Not a Substitute for Professional Legal Advice

For complex legal matters, we recommend consulting with a qualified lawyer. SamvidhanAI cannot represent you in court or provide attorney-client privileged communication.

Data Usage & Privacy

Your consultation data is stored securely on Indian servers. We use this data to improve our AI models and provide better service. Your data will never be sold to third parties.
